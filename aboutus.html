Welcome to Thao Realty

Founded in 2000, Thao Realty has been servicing the Minneapolis Saint Paul area for over 20 years. We have sold over 500,000 homes in the Minneapolis, MN area in that time. All of our homes in the last 2 years have sold for over 30% above market value. If you are looking to buy or sell a home we are the place for you.

Our team is led by Ho-Seng Thao who has been a realtor for over 35 years. He is married to his wife, Bao, and they just celebrated their 10 year anniversary. He has 4 kids, 2 boys and 2 girls. He is very passionate about selling homes.
