Welcome to Thao Realty

Founded in 202, Thao Realty has been servicing the Minneapolis Saint Paul area for 1 year. We have sold over 50,000 homes in the Minneapolis, MN area in that short time. All of our homes we have sold were over 10% above market value. If you are looking to buy or sell a home we are the place for you.

Our team is led by Ho-Seng Thao who has been a realtor for over 2 years. He is married to his wife, Bao, and they just celebrated their 10th wedding anniversary. He has 4 kids, 2 boys and 2 girls. He is very passionate about selling homes and software development.
